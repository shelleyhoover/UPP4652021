    Resize & Position Elements

    5 position properties:

    • Static: default. Elements follow a “normal flow” of top-down
    • Relative: will position div to new location based relative from its “normal” position.
    • Fixed: remains in the same position even if scrolling down the page.
    • Absolute: brings the element back to the beginning of the document flow. (Relative to a parent element)
    • Sticky: mix of fixed/relative. Will stick when reaching a certain point. (not supported in internet explorer and some older web browsers)
